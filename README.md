## 🌟 The Universe is No Longer Silent

In vanilla _X4: Foundations_, you fly in a mechanical masterpiece. Every trade ship, laser tower, and station is simulated in real time. Yet, the factions themselves feel like cold spreadsheets. They wage wars without memory, sign peace treaties without reason, and treat you as nothing more than a reputation score.

**AI Influence** changes the galaxy forever.

This mod connects the live X4 universe to the **Player2 AI companion** on your PC. It breathes real-time intelligence, memory, and personality into faction representatives, admirals, and station officers. They will speak to you in natural language, negotiate complex deals, explain their strategic decisions, and remember how you treat them.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🗣️ Conversational Comm-Link

Walk up to any faction representative (or hit `Shift + C` in space) to open a live comm-link. You can type messages in your own words or select context-aware dialogue suggestions generated on the fly.

### 🧠 Memories & Grudges

Factions keep track of your history. If you help defend an Argon sector under Xenon siege, their liaison will remember your heroism in future conversations. If you attack their civilian freighters, they won't forget—no matter how many credits you offer.

### 📈 Dynamic Negotiations

Negotiate terms directly. Ask for peace, trade access, or military protection. Factions will look at their current military losses, economic shortages, and relationship with you before giving you a realistic, narrative-driven counter-offer.

### 📰 Living Galactic News

Keep up with the galaxy. When a faction loses a capital ship, captures a sector, or experiences a critical resource shortage, they will publish statements, alerts, and articles in your in-game logbook, keeping you updated on the political climate.

## 🎮 How It Works (For Players)

You don't need a degree in software engineering to play this mod.

- When you talk to an NPC or type a message, the mod packages your game state (who you are, what sector you're in, and your current relation).
- The **Player2 AI** evaluates this state and the character's personality.
- The character responds to you in-character. If you reach an agreement (like paying credits for peace), the mod executes the change in your game safely.

## 🔒 Play Safe: Bounded AI & Save Protection

We know how much players value their long-term save files. AI Influence is built from the ground up to protect your game:
* **No Magic Resource Spawns:** The AI cannot spawn ships or resources out of thin air. Commands utilize existing simulated assets.
* **External Sandbox Memory:** Memories are stored in an external database, keeping your X4 save files clean, lightweight, and completely uncorrupted.
* **Idempotency Guard:** Every command carries a unique transaction ID, preventing duplicate credit deductions or relation shifts.
* **Fail-Safe Offline Mode:** If your AI bridge is offline or runs out of energy, the mod automatically falls back to canned, faction-appropriate dialogue templates. **No crashes, no freezes.**

## 🚀 Quick Setup Guide

### 📋 What You Need

1. **X4 Foundations** (v6.00 or newer).
2. **Player2 Desktop AI App** (installed and running on your PC).
3. The **X4 Neural Link** extension installed (manages the connection).
4. The **djfhe_http** extension installed (allows X4 to communicate with the bridge).

### 📥 Installation Steps

1. Place the `x4_ai_influence` folder into your X4 extensions folder:
   ```text
   X4 Foundations/extensions/x4_ai_influence
   ```
2. Double-click `Start-Neural-Link.bat` in the `x4_neural_link` folder.
3. Launch X4: Foundations.
4. Talk to any faction representative, click **"Speak to AI"**, and start shaping the future of the galaxy!

## ❓ Troubleshooting & Support

- **The "Speak to AI" option is greyed out/not appearing:** Make sure both the Player2 App and the Neural Link bridge (`Start-Neural-Link.bat`) are running in the background before you launch X4.
- **The representative doesn't reply:** Ensure your Player2 account has "joules" (AI power) available. If you are out of joules, the mod will fall back to local dialogue options automatically.
